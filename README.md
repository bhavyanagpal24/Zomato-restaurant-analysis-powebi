## 📊 Restaurant Performance & Customer Insight Dashboard
Analysed 6,984 restaurants from the Zomato dataset to uncover customer behaviour patterns, cuisine trends, and business performance insights using Power BI.
## 🔍 Key Insights
- North Indian and Chinese cuisines dominate with 3.2K and 2.4K outlets — indicating highest market demand
- 53% of restaurants offer online ordering, and they rate slightly higher (3.6 vs 3.5) — suggesting online presence improves customer perception
- Majority of restaurants (2,718) fall in the 3–4 rating range — market is moderately satisfied but has room for improvement
- Average cost for two is ₹543 — useful for pricing strategy benchmarking
## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI  
- Power Query (Data Cleaning & transformation )  
- DAX (Calculations & Measures)  

## 📁 Dataset
Zomato  dataset sourced from Kaggle
